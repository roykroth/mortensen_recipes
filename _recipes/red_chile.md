---
layout: recipe
title: Red Chile

ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 3 Tbsp flour
- 3 Tbsp oil
- 4 Tbsp powdered chili
- 3 large glasses water
- garlic salt
- cumin
- chili seeds

directions:
-  Brown ground beef
-  Add oil to pan
-  Add flour and combine to make a roux
-  Add chili powder
-  Add water
-  Season to taste
