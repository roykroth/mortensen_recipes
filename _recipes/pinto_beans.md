---
layout: recipe
title: Pinto Beans

ingredients:
- 4 cups beans, dry
- 2 Tbsp oil
- 2 Tbsp salt
- 1 tsp garlic powder

directions:
- Clean and sort beans
- Add to pressure cooker
- Fill with water to 1 inch from top
- Bring to pressure and cook 20-25 minutes
- Release Steam

---
A true Mortensen Classic.
