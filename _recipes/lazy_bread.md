---
layout: recipe
title: Lazy Bread

ingredients:
- 4 1/2 cups flour
- 3 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 2 tsp salt
- 4 tsp shortening
- 2 cups water (warmer than warm)

directions:
- Combine all ingredients except water
- Add water to make a dough
- Roll out to 1/2 inch thick
- Cut into pieces
- Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes on each side
